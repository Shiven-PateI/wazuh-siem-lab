ANALYSIS
--------
Five failed logon attempts were detected in rapid succession 
on the Windows-Endpoint machine. The pattern is consistent 
with a brute force credential attack — an automated or 
manual attempt to gain unauthorized access by guessing 
passwords.

The MITRE ATT&CK mapping to T1078 (Valid Accounts) indicates 
an attacker may be attempting to authenticate using 
legitimate account names with incorrect passwords, which 
is a common initial access technique.

In a real enterprise environment this pattern would warrant:
1. Immediate account lockout review
2. Source IP investigation and potential blocking
3. Notification to the account owner
4. Escalation to Tier 2 if attempts continue
